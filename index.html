I AIN'T AFRAID OF NO YUPPIES!

Why join the fight to protect communities against rising housing costs?

Historically disenfranchised communities are disproportionately affected by rising housing costs, forcing residential and business communities to move by raising rent and tax prices.

Use this tool to determine if the rental price you are considering in the greater Philadelphia region is ethically aligned with working against inflating housing market prices and further contributing to gentrifying your community.

Evaluate Your Rent

HARD PASS

The rental asking price is ABOVE fair market rent as determined by HUD. If you agree to take this lease, at this price, you will be contributing to the economical impacts of gentrification. We understand that the house search process is frustrating and housing options can be limited but we urge you to consider how the impact of pushing out communities with historic roots is violence that we can stand against by collectively refusing to contribute to raising housing costs.

Thank you for using our tool to help guide your decision to be a thoughtful and ethical contributor to your community!
